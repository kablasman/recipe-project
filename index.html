Delicious No-Knead Baguettes

Make three fluffy and delicious baguettes, using a simple
folding technique, instead of labor-intensive kneading.

  • Active Time 40 MINS

  • Total Time 3 HRS

  • Calories 1670

  • Skills Level MEDIUM

Ingredients

  • 500 g bread flour
  • 400 g cool water
  • 10 g salt
  • 7.5 g yeast
  • Extra flour (for dusting)

Equipment

  • Large bowl
  • Plastic wrap
  • Baking sheet/stone
  • Bench scraper
  • Thermometer
